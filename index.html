Blogger's Block

If there’s such a thing as bloggers block, it’s quite possible I’ve caught it. Either that or just plain laziness. The need to write something, anything, is there, but the mind turns up blank when attempting to manifest a jumble of occurrences and happenchance onto paper. So, I thoughts, let us conquer this bloggers block. Start by exploring something simple, and then move on to something even simpler.

Opoulos Conundrum

First, we’ll begin with the Opoulos conundrum. George Stroumboulopoulos, George Stephanopoulos, and George Christopoulos. Notice anything similar between these popular TV hosts names? Yep, you may have guessed it, but the rest of it is all Greek to me. The similarity is that they all contain ‘George’ and ‘Opoulos’, which at first glance seems kind of odd. How is it that three broadcasters all share the same first names, and almost identical last names?
